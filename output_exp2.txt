OBSERVATIONS — FIRST PING
  • ICMP and ARP both appeared at 0.000 sec — ICMP triggered ARP immediately.
  • Switch flooded the ARP broadcast to all ports (PC1 and PC2 both received it).
  • Only PC1 replied to the ARP Request since its IP address matched.
  • Switch dynamically learned MAC addresses of PC0 and PC1 from the traffic.
  • ICMP Echo Request was sent only after ARP resolution was complete (0.004 sec).
  • Total events recorded: 21+ (combination of ARP and ICMP packets).
